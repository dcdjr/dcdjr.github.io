About Me

I’m a computer science student at URI, pursuing the 4+1 Computer Science to Cybersecurity pathway as a Schilling Scholar and Honors student. I’m especially interested in cybersecurity and low-level systems, and I like turning what I learn into real, hands-on projects.

Some highlights so far include completing Harvard’s CS50, building software projects in C and Python, and working with the RI Air National Guard on a research project studying VR-based military training. I prefer learning by building, experimenting, breaking things, fixing them, and understanding how they work.

Long term, I want to work where security, engineering, and real systems intersect. I’m always open to internships, collaborations, and opportunities to grow while contributing meaningful work.
